One Pan Creamy Gnocchi with Sausage

Description

This creamy gnocchi with sausage recipe is an easy, quick, and incredibly delicious comfort food meal made all in one pan. It's ready in just 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 16 Oz Italian Sausage
  • Half an Onion
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/3 Cup of White Wine
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 Pound Gnocchi
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Handful Fresh Basil
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Sauté the sausage and onion in a skillet over medium-high heat for 7-10 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  2. Add the garlic to the pan and sauté for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the wine and let it cook for about a minute.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes, cream, and gnocchi. Once the sauce starts to bubble again, cover the pan and reduce the heat to medium. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Uncover the pan, give it a good stir, and let it cook for another few minutes, or until the gnocchi has cooked through and is nice and soft and the sauce has thickened to your liking.
  6. Stir in the parmesan cheese, basil, and salt & pepper, to taste. Serve immediately.
